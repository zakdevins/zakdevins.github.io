Ola Palczewski

What I desire most in my paintings is truth. Obsession comes and goes, and inspirations change; but truth must be absolute. It is in something as simple and complex as color, which has driven me to obsession recently. The physicality of the human body along with the landscapes we visit in our memories have been reoccurring themes in my most recent paintings. Above all, I am in love with the color of everyday life and the intimacy of the human form, and the extraordinarily ordinary.
